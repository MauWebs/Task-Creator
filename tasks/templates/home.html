Django App

This is a "CRUD" application created with "Python" and its framework "Django", in which you can create, read, update and delete tasks. You can also register with your own account with a password and a user name. Once registered you can log in again and close it whenever you want. Within the application you can view the tasks you have created and completed. As an additional feature you can highlight important tasks.
